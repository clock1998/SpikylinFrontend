About Me

This Website

This is an experimental-solo project. It allows me to try, experiment, and practice with all kinds of new technologies. And, the website is entirely done by myself, from the back-end to the web-design part.

My Mission

My mission, for now, is to build a Frankenstein-website that includes a set of small tools and utilities that may or may not be useful for my users or visitors. If you could find something helpful during your visit, that is great, just keep using it!

My Thoughts
